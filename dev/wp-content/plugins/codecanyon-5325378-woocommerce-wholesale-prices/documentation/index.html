A) Installation - top

Extract woo-wholesale-pricing.zip and upload to your wordpress plugin directory. Once uploaded visit your plugins page and activate "WooCommerce Wholesale Pricing"

Once installed every simple product will then have a wholsale price field added to the product data tab. For variable products, the wholesale price fields will be add to the variations tab within the product data tab.

Once these have been set with custom prices, they will then ovveride all default prices within your shop as long as you have a user role of "wholesale_customer" Wholesale Customer. The plugin adds this role on installation.
